About Me

I hold a background in physics with concentrated experience in computational materials science. My research approach combines rigorous simulation, methodical analysis, and clear documentation, supported by Python-driven automation and version-controlled workflows. Research Interests

Developing computational approaches to understand and predict materials behavior, focusing on structure, stability, and performance in technologically relevant contexts. Emphasis on reproducible workflows and robust methodological design.

Applying DFT and related methods to examine band structures, density of states, and surface phenomena. Interested in exploring methodological improvements and their impact on physical interpretation.
